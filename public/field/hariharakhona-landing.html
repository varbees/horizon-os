Why you can trust it

Three layers,
never blurred

Every plant carries three kinds of knowledge — kept strictly separate. This is what makes it a record, not wellness folklore.

Layer 01 · Cited
Classical

What Charaka, Sushruta, and the nighantus actually say — always with a sutra citation. The documented record, traceable to the source text.

Layer 02 · Attributed
Folk

What the Yanadi, Yerukala, and Chenchu communities actually practice — recorded with who said it, when, and the exact preparation. Living knowledge.

Layer 03 · Observed
Embodied

What a documenter saw with their own eyes, on a dated walk. First-person field record — never presented as advice, always as observation.
