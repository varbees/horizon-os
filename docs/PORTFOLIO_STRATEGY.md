# Portfolio Strategy: Revenue First Doctrine

## The North Star
Turn work into recurring income over a low burn, faceless, with no sales calls, no jobs, and no paid ads. 

**ONE Revenue Engine**: PhotoSelect (B2B SaaS for Indian wedding/event studios).
**ONE Fast-Cash Lane**: open-core developer products under `varbees` (starting with rateguard).

Current Milestone: **First paying studio → first 5 studios** for PhotoSelect. Everything not directly watering those two lanes is parked.

## Project Hierarchy

### 1. Active Money (Execute Daily)
*   **PhotoSelect** (`01-revenue/photoselect`): The primary engine. All focus is on unblocking the live Razorpay/WhatsApp credentials and securing the first paying studios.
*   **rateguard** (`02-fast-cash/rateguard`): The varbees open-core fast cash play. Focus on open-sourcing the core and shipping a hosted dashboard spec.
*   **Horizon OS** (`horizon-os`): The local orchestrator and command center. Only built vertically to support the execution of PhotoSelect and varbees.

### 2. Strategic Proof (Parked)
These projects represent future potential but must not consume execution bandwidth until the Active Money milestone is hit.
*   **Agent Linux Control**: Future demo asset.
*   **Antharmaya Labs**: The overarching entity; update only when PhotoSelect or RateGuard has proof worth publishing.
*   **Norma Smart Spice Dispenser**: Physical product; parked until a buyer truth is verified.
*   **PlantSage / ff-planter**: Strategic Stage-2 asset. Keep consistency bounded. Do not chase product polish while PhotoSelect and RateGuard are unpaid.
*   **SecureClaw**: Research asset.

### 3. Resurrect Candidates (Harvest)
*   **InvoAI / Billing Copilot**: Extract billing UX patterns when PhotoSelect's paid UI is due.
*   **Layers Green AI Studio**: Extract dashboard and SDK language for RateGuard; leave the standalone app parked.

### 4. Archive (Reference Only)
*   **A Game of Self, Adhi projects, Agent Skills, Anthar Vault, Antharmaya Premortem, deskold, dialysissaathi**: Kept strictly for referencing past code, architecture patterns, or specific components. No active development allowed.

## The Operating Loop
*   **Service Income First**: Cash floor must be maintained before any product sprawl.
*   **Backbone**: Body + Attention + Capital + Spec are foundational habits, not optional.
*   **Discipline**: If it doesn't unblock a PhotoSelect pilot or a RateGuard dollar, it gets queued in Horizon OS and parked.
