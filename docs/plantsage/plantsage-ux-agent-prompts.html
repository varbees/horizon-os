03 — Map UX Reference

Better than Google Earth.
Simpler than Snapchat.

The map is the game world. It needs to feel alive — not like a data layer on top of satellite imagery, but like the forest itself rendered as information. Here's what to take, avoid, and build toward.

Google Earth
What to borrow
  • Smooth satellite imagery blend
  • Terrain extrusion / 3D hills
  • GPS trail breadcrumb rendering
  • Altitude contour reading
  • High-res zoom into specific rock faces
Take this
Snapchat Map
What to borrow
  • Realtime presence dot — you're here, now
  • Zero-friction — map IS the home screen
  • Friend/community layer light touch
  • Tap a hotspot, something reveals
  • Live activity pulses around you
Take this
PlantSage Map
What we build
  • Forest floor green base, not grey city tiles
  • Species dots pulse when you're in range
  • Trail you've walked glows amber behind you
  • Undiscovered zones are darker, misted
  • Tap a dot → card flip right there on the map
  • Rainfall / season overlay (monsoon fungi mode)
Build this
THE ONE RULE FOR MAP UX

The map must make you want to walk further. Every design decision is evaluated against this single question. Darker undiscovered zones, pulsing undocumented species dots just off your trail, the amber glow of your own history — all of it exists to pull you 50m deeper into the forest than you planned.

04 — Motion Spec

Animation language

Every animation has a reason. The card flip is the moment of discovery — it deserves 600ms. The GPS dot pulse is ambient — it should never demand attention. Codex and Claude Code must know these values before generating any animated widget.

Interaction Animation Spec
Species identified Card flip 3D reveal + particle burst 600ms · cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1) · Lottie particles optional
GPS dot (you) Amber pulse ring expanding 2000ms loop · ease-in-out · ring opacity 0.8→0 · scale 1→2.5
Trail drawn behind you Amber path animates in behind footsteps Animates 200ms per GPS point · ease-out · PathMetrics Flutter
Undiscovered species dot Slow breathing pulse, green 3000ms loop · opacity 0.3↔0.8 · no scale change (non-intrusive)
Level up — Sage tier Full screen flash + title wipe 300ms flash → 800ms title slide up · Syne 800 font · Lottie burst
Streak fire (30+ days) Flame Lottie beside streak counter Ambient loop · 1.5s · scale with streak count
Card rarity reveal Rim light color change on card flip Common: leaf green · Rare: amber · Ancient: violet · Legendary: white flash
Camera scanning Horizontal scan line + corner brackets Scan line: 1200ms ease-in-out y-axis sweep · brackets: always visible
Forest mist (undiscovered zones) Subtle dark overlay on unexplored map areas Opacity 0.4 · lifts to 0 as GPS enters zone · 1500ms transition
Bottom sheet (species preview) Slide up from map tap 350ms · spring physics · dampingRatio 0.75 · stiffness 200
